Market Finder Tutorial (Google)

Find new international markets for your business with Google's data intelligence

1. What is Market Finder?

Market Finder is a free tool from Google that helps businesses to:

  • Identify new international markets
  • Receive personalized recommendations based on their website or products
  • Obtain data on demand, internet access, digital spending, and competition
Note: Ideal for digital or e-commerce companies in the internationalization phase.

2. Access to the tool

  1. Go to: Market Finder
  2. Select your language if desired
  3. Click "Get started"

You can use it with or without a Google account, although registration improves the experience.

3. How it works

  1. Enter the URL of your website
  2. Market Finder automatically detects your business categories
  3. It shows you a list of recommended countries based on your content and audience
  • You can also select categories manually
  • Each suggested market includes data such as search volume, estimated CPC, ease of doing business

4. Market evaluation

When exploring a country, you will see information about:

  • Search volume related to your products
  • Ease of doing business (Doing Business Score)
  • Estimated digital marketing costs
  • Consumer habits and internet access
Tip: You can save your favorite countries to compare them later.

5. Export plan

Once you have selected your target markets, the tool offers you:

  • Practical guides on localization, logistics, payments, and customer service
  • Resources on taxation and specific regulations
  • Suggestions for adapting Google Ads campaigns to each market

6. Practical example

Example: An online store of natural cosmetics

  1. Enter your store's website
  2. Market Finder detects "Cosmetics" as a category
  3. The suggested markets are Germany, Canada, and Chile
  4. You select Chile â†’ high search volume, medium CPC, and high mobile usage are shown
  5. It recommends adapting your site to Latin American Spanish and reviewing labeling regulations

7. Final recommendations

  • Use Market Finder as a starting point to explore digital markets
  • Complement with tools like Export Potential Map or Statista
  • Apply the insights in your SEM and SEO strategy for each market
