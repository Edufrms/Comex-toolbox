FAOLEX Database Tutorial

     

Consult agri-food and environmental legislation by country, sector, and date

   
   
     

1. What is FAOLEX?

     
       

FAOLEX is a legal database managed by the FAO (Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations), containing:

       
             
  • National laws, regulations, treaties, and policies
    •          
  • Topics: agriculture, fisheries, food, biodiversity, plant and animal health
    •          
  • Official sources from over 200 countries and territories
    •        
       
          Note: It is constantly updated with translated and classified regulatory documents.        
     
   
   
     

2. Accessing the site

     
       
             
  1. Visit: FAOLEX Country Profiles
    2.          
  2. You can also access it from the general page
    3.          
  3. The interface is available in several languages, including English
    4.        
     
   
   
     

3. How to search for legislation

     
       
             
  1. Click on “Country Profiles”
    2.          
  2. Select a country from the map or the alphabetical list
    3.          
  3. Choose a category: Agriculture, Food, Water, Environment, Fisheries, etc.
    4.        
       

The page will display a list of national laws with the date, type of regulation, and access to the original document or translation.

     
   
   
     

4. Filters and categories

     
       

The regulations are classified by:

       
             
  • Type: law, regulation, policy, treaty
    •          
  • Publication date
    •          
  • Language
    •          
  • Specific topic: biotechnology, food safety, fisheries law, agricultural trade
    •        
       
          Tip: Use keywords in English or Spanish to improve your results.        
     
   
   
     

5. Practical example

     
       

Example: Consulting legislation on food labeling in Mexico

       
             
  1. Select “Mexico” on the map
    2.          
  2. Filter by sector: “Food and Nutrition”
    3.          
  3. Click on a regulation like “Norma Oficial Mexicana NOM-051”
    4.          
  4. Read the summary and access the original document in PDF
    5.        
       

This is useful for exporters, regulatory consultants, or researchers in the agri-food sector.

     
   
   
     

6. Final recommendations

     
       
             
  • Consult FAOLEX before exporting food, beverages, or agro-industrial products
    •          
  • Verify if there are technical standards or health restrictions in force at the destination
    •          
  • Use it as a complement to Market Access Map or Access2Markets
    •
