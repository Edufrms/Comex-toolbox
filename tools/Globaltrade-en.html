Global Trade Helpdesk Tutorial

Find market opportunities, trade requirements, tariffs and key contacts in one place

1. What is Global Trade Helpdesk?

It is a free platform developed by ITC, UNCTAD and WTO that centralizes international trade information to support small and medium-sized enterprises in exporting.

  • Identify export opportunities by country and product
  • Check technical, health and tariff requirements
  • Access trade statistics
  • Find institutions that can support your business
Note: It integrates data from over 11 international organizations such as the World Bank, ITC, WTO, OECD and more.

2. Access the platform

  1. Visit: globaltradehelpdesk.org/en
  2. On the homepage, enter the product, exporting country and destination country
  3. Click “Search” to generate the full trade report

3. Information provided

  • Market opportunities: Demand level, top importing countries and competitors
  • Market access: Tariffs, taxes and sanitary regulations
  • Trade statistics: Export and import figures over recent years
  • Key contacts: Authorities, chambers and institutional points of contact
Tip: Click each tab in the left sidebar to explore the detailed sections of the report.

4. Practical example

Example: Exporting coffee from Colombia to Germany

  1. Type "coffee" or "0901" in the product field
  2. Select "Colombia" as the exporting country and "Germany" as the destination country
  3. Click "Search"
  4. Explore sections like opportunities, requirements and contacts

You will see that Germany is one of the top coffee importers and what certifications are required.

5. Recommendations

  • Use keywords or HS codes for more accurate results
  • Download the report in PDF to share with partners or include in presentations
  • Combine this tool with others like Trade Map or MACMap
