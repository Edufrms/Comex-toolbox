UN Comtrade Plus Tutorial

Access global international trade statistics by product, country, and year from official UN sources

1. What is UN Comtrade Plus?

It is the United Nations trade data platform, which provides official and harmonized information on imports and exports between countries, including:

  • Trade flows by product (HS/SITC codes)
  • Data by reporting and partner country
  • Annual and monthly statistics
  • Visualizations and database downloads
Note: The tool allows access to millions of records and generation of personalized queries with advanced filters.

4. Visualization and download

Tip: Use the filters by value, unit, or product code to obtain more precise insights.

  • Always verify the units of measure and data coverage by country
