UN Comtrade Plus Tutorial

Access global international trade statistics by product, country, and year from official UN sources

1. What is UN Comtrade Plus?

It is the United Nations trade data platform, which provides official and harmonized information on imports and exports between countries, including:

  • Trade flows by product (HS/SITC codes)
  • Data by reporting and partner country
  • Annual and monthly statistics
  • Visualizations and database downloads
Note: The tool allows access to millions of records and generation of personalized queries with advanced filters.

2. How to access?

  1. Visit: comtradeplus.un.org
  2. No registration is required to consult public data
  3. Use the “Build Query” button to create a custom query

3. How to build a query

  1. Click on “Build Query”
  2. Select the flow type: imports, exports, or both
  3. Choose the countries (reporter and partner)
  4. Select the classification system (HS 2017, SITC, etc.)
  5. Enter the product (by name or code)
  6. Indicate the years and click on “Run Query”

The system will show you an interactive table with the results.

4. Visualization and download

  • The results can be viewed as a table, graph, or map
  • You can download the data in CSV or JSON
  • You can also copy links to share your queries
Tip: Use the filters by value, unit, or product code to obtain more precise insights.

5. Practical example

Example: Cocoa exports from Ivory Coast to Spain

  1. Flow type: Exports
  2. Reporter country: Ivory Coast
  3. Partner country: Spain
  4. HS product code: 1801 (cocoa beans)
  5. Year: 2022
  6. Click on “Run Query”

The system will show FOB value, volume, destination country, and trends by year.

6. Final recommendations

  • Comtrade is ideal for competitive analysis or global flow reports
  • Use it along with TradeMap to enrich your graphs or justify market strategies
  • Always verify the units of measure and data coverage by country
