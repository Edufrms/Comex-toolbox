Export Potential Map Tutorial

Complete guide to identify export opportunities based on real data

1. What is Export Potential Map?

Export Potential Map is a tool from the International Trade Centre (ITC) that allows you to discover products and markets with high export potential, using trade, tariff, and supply and demand data.

  • Explore export diversification opportunities
  • Identify products with high untapped potential
  • Based on robust and transparent methodology
Note: It is especially useful for SMEs, governments, and trade promotion agencies.

2. How to access

  1. Visit exportpotential.intracen.org/en
  2. No registration is required to use most of its features
  3. You can change the language (available in English, French, and Spanish)

3. Search by country

From the main screen, choose an exporting country to see:

  • Its main export opportunities
  • Potential markets by product
  • Data on demand, supply, and geographical proximity
Tip: You can filter by categories such as food, textiles, machinery, etc.

4. Search by product

Select a specific product to visualize:

  • The countries with the highest import potential
  • The untapped potential
  • Existing competition in the target market
Important: Use the search bar with keywords or HS codes (Harmonized System).

5. Opportunity explorer

Functionality that allows you to identify country-product combinations with the greatest potential:

  1. Select an exporting country
  2. Observe products with high untapped potential
  3. Explore emerging markets for each product

6. Interpretation of results

The graphs show:

  • Export potential (maximum estimated value)
  • Current actual exports
  • Untapped potential
Note: Indicators such as stability, global demand, and ease of access are also presented.

7. Practical example

Export of cocoa from Colombia

  • Select Colombia as the exporting country
  • Search for "cocoa" or enter the HS code
  • Identify countries like Germany, Belgium, or the Netherlands as destinations with high potential
  • Compare the current exported value with the estimated potential
Tip: You can export the data or generate graphs for presentations.

8. Final tips

  • Check the tool regularly for updates
  • Combine it with TradeMap for a complete analysis
  • Include the results in your internationalization strategy
