Iberglobal Tutorial

Access market studies, country reports, and specialized resources for business internationalization.

1. What is Iberglobal?

Iberglobal is a reference portal for internationalization information for companies. It offers market studies, country analysis, risk assessments, and support documents for Spanish exporters and investors.

  • Sectoral and foreign market studies
  • International economic outlook reports
  • Country risk assessments
  • News and updates on foreign trade and investments
Note: Many resources come from organizations like ICEX, World Bank, IMF, and private consultancies.

2. How to access the platform

  1. Visit: iberglobal.com
  2. Explore the different sections: market studies, country reports, risk alerts, etc.

3. Key content

  • Market studies: Analysis of key sectors and countries for internationalization.
  • Strategic reports: Updated information on global trends.
  • Risk documents: Country risk assessments to support investment decisions.
  • Practical guides: Resources to assist export and international expansion processes.
Tip: Use the internal search tool to filter documents by region, sector, or resource type.

4. Practical example

Example: Searching for risk information for Latin America

  1. Go to the "Country Risk" section.
  2. Filter by region (Latin America) or specific country.
  3. Review the latest reports from organizations like the World Bank or CESCE.

These reports help you anticipate political, economic, and financial risks in international markets.

5. Recommendations

  • Check updated reports regularly to stay informed about changes in target markets.
  • Complement Iberglobal's information with databases like UNCTADstat, Trade Map, or Access2Markets.
  • Use the resources to build stronger, well-informed international market entry strategies.
