Business Resources Tutorial - Junta de Castilla y León

     

Access aid, services, training, and support for businesses from the official website

   
   
     

1. What is this portal?

     
       

The official website of the Junta de Castilla y León for businesses centralizes information on:

       
             
  • Regional grants and aid
    •          
  • Support for internationalization and export
    •          
  • Digitalization, innovation, and sustainability
    •          
  • Training and advice for entrepreneurs
    •        
       
          Note: It is aimed at the self-employed, SMEs, and large companies based in Castilla y León.        
     
   
   
     

2. Accessing the website

     
       
             
  1. Visit: empresas.jcyl.es
    2.          
  2. Access the left sidebar menu to explore key areas
    3.          
  3. You can also use the general website search engine
    4.        
     
   
   
     

3. Featured sections

     
       
             
  • Grants and subsidies: open and previous calls
    •          
  • Internationalization: support for fairs, missions, and ICE advice
    •          
  • R&D&i: incentives for digitalization, industry 4.0, and sustainability
    •          
  • Training: free programs and specialized training
    •        
       
          Tip: Use the filters by sector, province, or type of aid to find relevant programs.        
     
   
   
     

4. How to apply for aid

     
       
             
  1. Go to the “Ayudas y subvenciones” (Grants and Subsidies) section
    2.          
  2. Select the current call of your interest
    3.          
  3. Download the terms and requirements
    4.          
  4. Access the “Tramitación electrónica” (Electronic Processing) link
    5.          
  5. You will need a digital certificate or Cl@ve
    6.        
       

Some calls are managed directly by the ICE (Institute for Business Competitiveness).

     
   
   
     

5. Practical example

     
       

Example: Applying for aid to attend an international fair

       
             
  1. Go to “Internacionalización” (Internationalization)
    2.          
  2. Select “Convocatoria para ferias internacionales 2025” (Call for international fairs 2025)
    3.          
  3. Review requirements: be an SME, have an exportable product, etc.
    4.          
  4. Download the application form
    5.          
  5. Submit electronically with a digital signature
    6.        
     
   
   
     

6. Recommendations

     
       
             
  • Subscribe to the ICE newsletter to not miss any calls
    •          
  • Check the training section to take advantage of free workshops
    •          
  • Use this portal as a basis to complement with ICEX, CEOE, or Chambers of Commerce
    •
