ICEX Statistics Tutorial

Access official data on exports and imports from Spain by product, country, and sector

1. What is the ICEX Statistics module?

It is a commercial data query tool developed by ICEX Spain Export and Investment. It allows you to visualize foreign trade statistics at a very detailed and segmented level.

  • Export and import data from/to Spain
  • Breakdown by country, product, autonomous community, company, and sector
  • Monthly update based on data from the Tax Agency
Note: This tool is free and does not require prior registration.

4. Customizing results

Once the query is made, you can:

6. Advantages and limitations

  • Advantages: precise data, official source, segmentation by company (in advanced version)
  • Limitations: does not include unit prices or commercial margins; does not have cartographic visualization
  • Ideal complement: combine with TradeMap, Export Potential Map, and Market Access Map

7. Usage recommendations

  • Update your searches every month: data is usually published between the 20th and 25th
  • Save your favorite combinations in Excel for future monitoring
  • Use the results to support presentations, SWOT analyses, or sector analyses
