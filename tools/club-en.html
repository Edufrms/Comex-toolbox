Spanish Exporters and Investors Club Tutorial

Access strategic analyses, market studies, and activities supporting business internationalization.

1. What is the Spanish Exporters and Investors Club?

The Spanish Exporters and Investors Club is a private business association grouping companies active in internationalization, foreign trade, and global investments. It provides strategic analyses, economic reports, and facilitates networking among internationally active companies.

  • Studies and reports on foreign trade and investment
  • Policy positioning before administrations and international organizations
  • Networking activities and specialized training
Note: The Club represents its members before public institutions to promote policies supporting internationalization.

2. How to access the platform

  1. Visit: clubexportadores.org
  2. Explore sections like reports, events, and institutional activities.

3. Key content

  • Strategic reports: Analyses of international environments for exporters and investors.
  • Events: Conferences, seminars, and internationalization forums.
  • Institutional representation: Defense of business interests before public bodies.
  • Support resources: Reference documents, studies, and publications.
Tip: Check the "Reports" section to download strategic documents without registration.

4. Practical example

Example: Downloading an internationalization report

  1. Go to the "Documentation" or "Publications" section.
  2. Look for recent studies on business internationalization or political risks.
  3. Download the documents of interest for free.

These reports are useful for preparing expansion strategies and identifying risks in foreign markets.

5. Recommendations

  • Subscribe to alerts to receive notifications about new events and reports.
  • Participate in forums and networking meetings to strengthen international business relationships.
  • Complement this information with resources such as Iberglobal, ICEX, or Access2Markets.
