International Trade Hub

Find in one place the best tools for foreign trade: market access, export statistics, market intelligence and international regulations. Ideal for exporters, importers, consultants and students.

Who I am

ComexToolbox is a project created by a professional in the agri-food sector specialized in foreign trade and digital business with the mission to bring together the best digital tools for foreign trade professionals, entrepreneurs, students and anyone else who wants to know how international trade works. This platform was born from direct experience with useful but scattered resources, and the need to have everything in one place.

What is ComexToolbox

ComexToolbox is a digital directory of tools and resources to streamline work in the field of foreign trade. ComexToolbox brings together in a single webpage all the tools currently used to face international trade challenges through market knowledge and other resources. It allows you to have a large amount of resources in an orderly, classified, and detailed way in one place to save time, improve decision-making through a more technical view of the markets, and discover new tools. The Foreign Trade Hub provides links and explains each tool from experience and sector knowledge.

Who it is intended for

At first it was a personal project for my own use, but after the interest of people close to me I decided to share my tool freely for any professional in the sector, students or teachers.

Why it was created

The idea came after the favorites bar of my browser collapsed with tools on market research, trade flows, tariffs, logistics... and when it came to the truth I did not know which one to use or was not able to exploit its true potential. So I wanted to create a resource that would automate my work and give me a head start in the industry.

What makes it different

ComexToolbox not only lists tools: we explain them step by step. In addition, all content is in English and Spanish, accessible without registration and open to suggestions from the community.

My vision

My vision is to create an automated platform that allows to extract data and information from all the tools quickly and easily in the Trade Hub itself. A reliable database, whose data has been obtained from official and truthful websites. A Swiss army knife, many tools in one.
